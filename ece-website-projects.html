ECE Website Projects

My Role: Website Production Assistant at Edward S. Rogers Sr. Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Toronto, Hired July 2024.

The department offers undergraduate and graduate programs in electrical and computer engineering.



Goals + Impact

The aim of the role was to support student recruitment and streamline the website experience by:
  • Enhancing usability and accessibility
  • Redesigning key pages to accurately portray student life
  • Making departmental information easier to access

Concise Design Process

Responsibilities:
  • Audited and optimized web pages for usability and accessibility
  • Redesigned key web pages using Figma and WordPress
  • Collaborated across the department to update content and media
  • Led the research and deployment of website design tasks

Results

Successfully led the design of several key website pages, improving the user experience for prospective and current students while supporting the department's recruitment and communications goals.
