ECE Website Projects

My Role: Website Production Assistant at Edward S. Rogers Sr. Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Toronto, Hired July 2024.

The department offers undergraduate and graduate programs in electrical and computer engineering.



Goals + Impact

The aim of the role was to support student recruitment and streamline the website experience by:
  • Enhancing usability and accessibility
  • Redesigning key pages to accurately portray student life
  • Making departmental information easier to access

Concise Design Process

Responsibilities:
  • Audited and optimized web pages for usability and accessibility
  • Redesigned key web pages using Figma and WordPress
  • Collaborated across the department to update content and media
  • Led the research and deployment of website design tasks

Results

Successfully led the design of several key website pages, improving the user experience for prospective and current students while supporting the department's recruitment and communications goals.

ECE Department Homepage ↗

Full Redesign & Development | January - March 2025

I redesigned and developed the department’s homepage to focus on prospective student recruitment. The new layout enhances navigation, prioritizes key academic, research, and student life content, and aligns with the department’s goals.



ECE Events Page ↗

Full Redesign & Development | March 2025

I expanded the events page beyond the calendar widget by adding introductory content and highlighting three main event categories. This helped make the page more informative and welcoming while improving clarity around departmental activities.



Toronto Nanofabrication Centre (TNFC) (Page under construction)

Migration of Existing Site | Ongoing 2025

I led the migration of the Toronto Nanofabrication Centre’s standalone website into the main ECE site. The redesign preserved key content while updating layouts and improving consistency with the department’s design system.



Student Research Opportunities ↗

Design & Development | December 2025

I created this brand-new subpage under the Research section to encourage student participation in faculty-led research. It features persuasive content and showcases available positions for undergraduate and graduate research assistants.
